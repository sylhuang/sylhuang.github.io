About

Sylvia Huang is a second-year student in Computer Science and Biology program at Mcgill University. Currently, she is working with Dr. Alanna Watt in the Watt Lab.

Inspired by her Neuroscience and Brain Enhancement pre-college course during summer, 2014, Sylvia has always been interested in Brain-to-Computer/Brain-to-Brain Interface.
